Whitewater Rafting

At White Water Rafting, we believe everyone should experience the most exciting adventure that nature has to offer. Whether you're an experienced rafter or just starting out, we have the perfect trips lined up for you!

History

What began as a few friends chasing rapids on old inner tubes in the summer of '86 has grown into one of the most trusted white water rafting outfitters in the region. Fueled by a passion for adventure and a deep respect for the river, we officially launched Whitewater Rafting in 1992 with one raft, one truck, and a wild dream.

Since then, we've guided thousands of thrill-seekers down winding canyons and through churning rapids, all while staying true to our roots: safety, fun, and a little splash of mischief. From modest beginnings to award-winning tours, our story is written in every wave we ride—and we're just getting started.
